My Favorite Places!

Tintagel Castle: Tintagel, Cornwall.
Located in Cornwall, England; it is a 1,500 year old castle that is said to be the birthplace of King Arthur.

Cadbury Castle: Somerset, England
Located in Somerset, England, Cadbury Castle is an Iron Age fort that many believe to be the location of Camelot, King Arthur's castle.

Glastonbury Tor: Somerset, England
Located in Somerset, England, the tower of Glastonbury (also known as 'the Tor') is surrounded by many legends, ranging from the birthplace of many Celtic Myths, as well as the entrance to Avalon, where King Arthur slumbers.
