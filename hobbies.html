My Hobbies!

Studying! A modern man's weapon for success!
When I have nothing to do, I usually start studying lessons related to the subjects I am taking. As a wise man once said, knowing is only half the battle.

Reading! A gateway to another world!
As a programmer, and someone with a wild imagination, reading stories gives me a place to escape, and act as my sandbox to play out different worlds, and different scenarios. Sometimes, they even give me ideas for new projects to do as well.

Singing! When sometimes, you gotta let everything out!
Singing has always interested me ever since I was a small child. After a good number of obstacles, I have finally given myself the confidence to use my voice, and let out everything I keep bottled up inside with the songs I love.
